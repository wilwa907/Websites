About Us

Wilwa's Burger is a restaurant that was founded 2023 in Sweden. Today we have several restaurants in Sweden and one in Oslo, Norway. At Wilwa's Burger all the employees shares the genuine interest of food and especially burgers. Here at Wilwa's Burger we values burgers made of fresh ingredients.

Try our "Burger of the Day" or any of the other regular burgers on the menu. Feel free to either come and visit any of our cozy restaurants or order online to enjoy a lovely burger at your place.
